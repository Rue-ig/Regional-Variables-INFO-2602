About DWIVE

Your Caribbean events aggregator — built in Trinidad, for the region.

flagOur Mission

DWIVE was built to solve a real problem: Caribbean events are scattered across dozens of ticketing sites, social media pages, and word-of-mouth. We pull it all together into one clean, searchable platform so you never miss what's happening on your island — or the next one over.

From carnival fetes in Trinidad to jazz festivals in Saint Lucia, we aggregate, verify, and present events from across the Caribbean so you can browse, discover, and plan with ease.

buildHow it works

  • Our scrapers pull event data from Eventbrite, Ticketmaster, islandetickets.com, trinijunglejuice.com, and more — automatically, every 12 hours.
  • Every event is reviewed by our admin team before it goes live.
  • Community members can submit events, leave reviews, and upload photos.
  • Promoters can contact us directly to have their events featured.
