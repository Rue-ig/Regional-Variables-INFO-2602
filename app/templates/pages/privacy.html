Privacy Policy

How DWIVE collects, uses, and protects your information.

calendar_todayLast updated: April 2026

infoOverview

DWIVE ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at dwive.tt. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. By using DWIVE you agree to the terms of this policy.

databaseData we collect

  • Account data: username, email address, and hashed password when you register.
  • User-generated content: reviews, star ratings, photos, and event bookmarks you create.
  • Session data: a secure HTTP-only cookie to keep you logged in.
  • Usage data: standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) for security and debugging — retained for 30 days.

We do not collect payment information, precise geolocation, or government IDs.

settingsHow we use your data

  • Authenticating your account and securing your session.
  • Displaying your reviews and photos on event pages (with your username).
  • Sending transactional emails such as password reset links (when implemented).
  • Improving platform performance and diagnosing errors via server logs.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

cookieCookies

We use a single first-party session cookie (access_token) to maintain your login state. No third-party tracking cookies are set. See our Cookie Policy for details.

gavelYour rights

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: update your username or email from your profile page.
  • Deletion: request full account deletion by emailing hello@dwive.tt.
  • Portability: request your data in a machine-readable format.

lockData security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage. Session tokens are signed with a server-side secret key. The database is not exposed to the public internet. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

mailContact

For privacy-related enquiries contact hello@dwive.tt or use our contact form.
