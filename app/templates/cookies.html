Cookie Policy

A clear explanation of every cookie DWIVE uses.

infoWhat are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit — such as keeping you logged in — so you don't have to re-enter details on every page.

listCookies we use

Cookie name Type Purpose Duration
access_token First-party, essential Stores your signed JWT so you remain authenticated between pages. Set as HTTP-only and Secure. Expires based on JWT_ACCESS_TOKEN_EXPIRES setting (default: 30 minutes).
session First-party, essential Starlette session middleware — stores flash messages and CSRF tokens. Browser session (cleared when browser closes).

DWIVE does not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics), or any third-party tracking cookies. The Eventbrite session cookies referenced in our scraper configuration are used exclusively for server-side data collection and are never sent to or stored in your browser.

blockManaging cookies

The cookies DWIVE sets are strictly necessary for the platform to function. Blocking them via your browser settings will prevent you from logging in. You can clear your cookies at any time through your browser's settings, which will log you out of DWIVE.

mailQuestions?

Contact us at hello@dwive.tt if you have questions about our cookie usage.
